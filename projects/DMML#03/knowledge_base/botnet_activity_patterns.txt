Botnet Activity Indicators

Overview
Botnets are networks of compromised machines controlled by attackers. These infected systems communicate with command servers to receive instructions.

Indicators
• Large numbers of background processes
• Network communication with unknown servers
• Periodic outbound connections

Associated MITRE Techniques
T1071 – Application Layer Protocol
T1105 – Ingress Tool Transfer

Attack Explanation
Botnet malware allows attackers to remotely control infected machines. These machines may participate in distributed attacks such as DDoS or spam campaigns.
